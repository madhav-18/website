Power Saving Heater

SMAJ’s specially designed and engineered the power saver heater to meet the ever increasing demand for energy conservation and to improve operations efficiency, capable of generating suitable temperature for processing resign with higher processing temperature. Energy Conservation is achieved by using thick Ceramic fiber insulating blanket on surface. Power consumption is reduced upto 30 percent because of low thermal conductivity of the ceramic fiber insulation. The external surface temperatures of the power saver band heaters are approximately 800 deg C
